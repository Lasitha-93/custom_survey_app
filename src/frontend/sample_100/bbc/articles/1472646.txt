Large parts of the UK have enjoyed a warm end to the Easter weekend - with temperatures peaking at almost 21C  in some areas of southern England.Usk in south Wales recorded a top temperature of 19.9C  on Monday, while many in England, Northern Ireland and other parts of Wales also got plenty of sunshine. However, the highest temperatures of the Easter weekend were recorded in Scotland, with those in Aboyne, Aberdeenshire, hitting 20.7C  on Sunday - the hottest day of the year so far.
