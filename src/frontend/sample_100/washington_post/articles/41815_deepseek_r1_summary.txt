A U.S. Coast Guard helicopter crew dramatically rescued four fishermen from a sinking vessel near Alaska Wednesday morning, releasing video of the operation Thursday. The 73-foot fishing boat *Kupreanof* was taking on water west of Juneau, prompting the crew, including one elderly man who couldn't swim, to abandon ship into a life raft. An MH-60 Jayhawk helicopter from Air Station Sitka located the mariners. Footage shows the perilous conditions as the helicopter hovered low over the ocean, carefully maneuvering to avoid blowing the life raft into the sinking vessel. The aircrew successfully hoisted all four men aboard the helicopter. As the rescue concluded, the *Kupreanof* disappeared beneath the waves.
