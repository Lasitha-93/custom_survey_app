Teen Book Club participants who missed out on the "How to Save a Life" giveaway can still engage through alternative options. Readers can purchase the book at a 20% discount via the Guardian Bookshop or borrow it free from local libraries. Upcoming activities include a video review competition offering a Kindle and YA books as prizes. Additional content features author Sara Zarr’s Top 10 Family Dramas list and access to the book’s first chapter. The club encourages feedback through its Facebook page or Twitter (@Guardianteenbks).
