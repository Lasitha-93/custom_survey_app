‘Of soil and water: King’s Cross Pond Club’ is part swimming pool, part art installation      The pond water is purified through a natural closed-loop process, using wetland and submerged water plants to filter and keep the water clear      The pond will be open for two years      It’s juxtaposed by the construction of King’s Cross      Visitors can book a time slot to swim in the pond      The pond is surrounded by pioneer plants, wild flowers grasses and bushes so that the environment evolves as the seasons change      The shower area.
