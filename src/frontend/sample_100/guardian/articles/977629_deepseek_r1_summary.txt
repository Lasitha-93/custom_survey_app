Richard Bedford has won a wildlife photography competition with his striking image "Fighting pheasants," capturing two birds mid-battle. The contest featured diverse avian subjects photographed across various locations. Notable entries included Kevin Du Rose's "Air-braking puffin" in dynamic flight, Dileep Kumar's "Greater spotted eagle" from Qatar, and Paul Hillion's "Gannets off Alderney." Other shortlisted images encompassed Mark Hancox's common kestrel pursuing a barn owl, Garth Peacock's male lesser-spotted woodpecker, and Jim Wood's short-eared owl. The submissions highlighted both behavioral moments—such as Tom Charles' drinking reed warbler and Graham Catley's displaying tree pipit—and technical skill in action shots like John Betts' pheasant in flight.
