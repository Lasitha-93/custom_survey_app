Major music festivals and concerts flourished globally in early July, featuring top artists across genres. Nicki Minaj performed in Paris, Madonna in Cologne, Germany, and Brian May with Adam Lambert in London. Jill Scott appeared at Rotterdam's North Sea Jazz festival. Despite challenging weather, crowds gathered at Scotland's T in the Park and Spain's Rock in Rio near Madrid. Switzerland's Openair festival in Frauenfeld hosted rapper Sido and enjoyed sunshine. In the US, Kanye West played Atlantic City while Mary J Blige headlined New Orleans' Essence festival. Spoek Mathambo performed in New York. Additional European stops included Bloc Party in Bilbao, Spain; Saint Saviour in Berlin; and Machine Head at Denmark's Roskilde festival. The events underscored a busy international summer concert schedule.
