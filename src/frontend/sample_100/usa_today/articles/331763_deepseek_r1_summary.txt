The McCaughey septuplets—Brandon, Joel, Nathan, Kenny Jr., Natalie, Alexis, and Kelsey—graduated Sunday with Carlisle High School's Class of 2016 in Des Moines, Iowa. Wearing black and red gowns, they walked across the stage during the ceremony on the school's football field, blending in with peers despite national fame since their birth in 1997. Alexis McCaughey was recognized as part of the National Honor Society, and Natalie McCaughey ranked in the top 15% academically. After receiving diplomas, the siblings celebrated with classmates before reuniting with parents Bobbi and Kenny for a group photo. The family declined recent media interviews. Last fall, the parents told The Des Moines Register that some septuplets may accept free college offers from Hannibal-LaGrange University in Missouri.
